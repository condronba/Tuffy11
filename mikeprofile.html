Music Man Mike


Music Man Mike Music Man Mike brings the tree house to life with songs and dance! From Poedy’s computer the trio of friends listen and move to new and exciting music every time they visit with him. Music Man Mike wears a different and silly hat for each unique song he shares. His music empowers everyone to “Get up off their seat and wiggle to the beat!”
